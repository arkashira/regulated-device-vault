# Business Model Canvas — regulated-device-vault

## Customer Segments
- **Class II/III medical device startups (Series A–C)** building software-driven devices (CGMs, infusion pumps, patient monitors, surgical robotics) — 5–50 engineers, no in-house RA/QA software tooling. Primary beachhead.
- **MedTech contract developers / design houses** (e.g. firms doing 510(k) and CE-MDR submissions for clients) who need repeatable, auditable build-to-validation pipelines across multiple device programs.
- **Regulatory & Quality (RA/QA) teams** inside mid-size OEMs ($50M–$500M revenue) drowning in manual traceability between requirements, code, and test evidence.
- **Notified Body / FDA-facing consultants** who resell a compliant toolchain to clients as part of submission packages.

## Value Propositions
- **"Audit-ready by default"** — automatic, immutable traceability from requirement → code → test → risk control, mapped to IEC 62304, ISO 14971, IEC 62366, FDA 21 CFR 820/QMSR. Cuts submission-prep time from months to weeks.
- **Embedded-to-cloud in one vault** — single validated SDLC spanning firmware (C/C++/RTOS), gateway, and cloud, instead of stitching 4–6 disconnected tools (Jira + Polarion + Git + manual Word DHF).
- **Reduce cost of a single audit finding** — a 483 / nonconformity or delayed submission costs a device firm $250K–$2M+ (THB ฿9M–฿73M) in burn; the vault de-risks that.
- **Pre-built compliance templates** for software safety classification (Class A/B/C), SOUP/OTS management, and cybersecurity (FDA premarket cyber guidance, IEC 81001-5-1).

## Channels
- **Direct founder-led sales** into MedTech accelerators (MedTech Innovator, Y Combinator bio, JLABS) and device incubators.
- **Regulatory consultant partner channel** — RA/QA consultancies embed the vault in client engagements (rev-share).
- **Content + SEO** on high-intent queries ("IEC 62304 traceability tool", "510(k) software documentation"); webinars with notified bodies.
- **Industry presence** — MD&M, BIOMEDevice, RAPS conferences; design partner pilots converted to references.

## Customer Relationships
- **High-touch onboarding + compliance success manager** for first 90 days (audits are existential; trust must be earned hands-on).
- **Self-serve docs + template library** for day-to-day engineering use once embedded.
- **Quarterly regulatory-change updates** pushed into the platform (e.g. EU MDR transition, FDA QMSR 2026 harmonization) — retention hook.
- **Validated audit-support SLA** — vault stands behind its evidence exports during a live FDA/NB audit.

## Revenue Streams
- **Per-program SaaS subscription** — $30K–$120K/yr (THB ฿1.1M–฿4.4M) per active device program, tiered by software safety class and seat count.
- **Platform validation package** (IQ/OQ/PQ documentation for the tool itself, required for regulated use) — $15K–$40K (THB ฿550K–฿1.5M) one-time per customer.
- **Submission-acceleration professional services** — $200–$350/hr (THB ฿7,300–฿12,800) for guided 510(k)/MDR evidence assembly.
- **Partner rev-share** from consultant-driven deals (15–25% margin pass-through).
- **Expansion**: per-additional-program and cybersecurity (SBOM/vuln-management) add-on module.

## Key Resources
- **Validated, regulatory-mapped platform & template engine** (core IP) — the traceability + evidence-export engine.
- **In-house regulatory/QA domain expertise** (ex-FDA reviewer or RA professional on team) — non-negotiable credibility.
- **The codebase + immutable audit-log infrastructure** (tamper-evident storage, e-signatures per 21 CFR Part 11).
- **Reference customers + passed-audit case studies** — the real moat in a trust-driven market.

## Key Activities
- **Maintaining regulatory mappings** as standards evolve (IEC 62304, ISO 14971, FDA QMSR, EU MDR/cyber).
- **Platform validation & verification** of our own tool to regulated-software grade.
- **Design-partner pilots** and converting them into audit-proven references.
- **Security & integrity engineering** — Part 11 e-signatures, access control, immutable logs.

## Key Partners
- **Regulatory consultancies & notified bodies** (channel + credibility).
- **Embedded toolchain vendors** (compiler/RTOS/static-analysis: e.g. LDRA, Parasoft, Green Hills) for integrated evidence capture.
- **Cloud + compliance infra** (HIPAA/SOC 2-ready hosting; HITRUST).
- **MedTech accelerators & device incubators** for top-of-funnel.

## Cost Structure
- **Engineering payroll** (embedded + cloud + security specialists) — dominant cost, $80K–$200K/yr loaded per senior eng (THB ฿2.9M–฿7.3M).
- **Regulatory/QA staff + ongoing standards licensing** (ISO/IEC standards are paid; ~$5K–$20K/yr, THB ฿180K–฿730K).
- **Tool self-validation & compliance audits (SOC 2, Part 11)** — $50K–$150K/yr (THB ฿1.8M–฿5.5M).
- **Cloud hosting + tamper-evident storage** — scales with program count, ~10–18% of revenue.
- **Sales & design-partner success** — long enterprise/regulated sales cycles (6–12 months) inflate CAC.
