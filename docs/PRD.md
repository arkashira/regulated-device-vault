# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Regulated Device Vault

### Overview

Regulated Device Vault is a platform designed to streamline the development and validation of regulated medical device software across the embedded-to-cloud stack. This product aims to simplify the complex regulatory landscape, ensuring compliance and reducing the time-to-market for medical device manufacturers.

### Problem Statement

Medical device manufacturers face significant challenges in developing and validating software for regulated devices. The current process is often manual, error-prone, and time-consuming, leading to delays in product launches and increased costs. The lack of a unified platform for managing regulatory requirements, testing, and validation exacerbates these issues.

### Target Users

* Medical device manufacturers (OEMs, contract manufacturers, and startups)
* Regulatory affairs teams
* Software development teams
* Quality assurance teams

### Goals

1. **Simplify Regulatory Compliance**: Provide a centralized platform for managing regulatory requirements, reducing the administrative burden on medical device manufacturers.
2. **Streamline Development and Validation**: Offer a unified environment for software development, testing, and validation, ensuring compliance and reducing the time-to-market.
3. **Enhance Collaboration**: Facilitate communication and collaboration between regulatory affairs, software development, and quality assurance teams.

### Key Features (Prioritized)

#### High Priority (Must-Haves)

1. **Regulatory Database**: A comprehensive database of regulatory requirements for medical devices, including FDA, EU MDR, and other global regulations.
2. **Software Development Environment**: A cloud-based IDE for developing, testing, and validating medical device software.
3. **Validation and Verification Tools**: Integrated tools for performing validation and verification activities, including risk analysis and testing.
4. **Collaboration and Workflow Management**: A platform for managing workflows, assigning tasks, and tracking progress across teams.

#### Medium Priority (Should-Haves)

1. **Integration with Existing Tools**: Seamless integration with popular development tools, such as Git, JIRA, and Jenkins.
2. **Automated Testing and Validation**: Automated testing and validation capabilities to reduce manual effort and improve accuracy.
3. **Reporting and Analytics**: Real-time reporting and analytics to track progress, identify bottlenecks, and optimize workflows.

#### Low Priority (Nice-to-Haves)

1. **Artificial Intelligence-powered Regulatory Compliance**: AI-driven recommendations for regulatory compliance, based on industry best practices and regulatory updates.
2. **Customizable Workflows**: Customizable workflows to accommodate specific business needs and regulatory requirements.

### Success Metrics

1. **Time-to-Market Reduction**: Measure the reduction in time-to-market for medical device manufacturers using the Regulated Device Vault platform.
2. **Regulatory Compliance Rate**: Track the rate of regulatory compliance achieved by manufacturers using the platform.
3. **User Adoption and Satisfaction**: Monitor user adoption rates and satisfaction levels to ensure the platform meets the needs of its users.

### Scope

The Regulated Device Vault platform will include the following components:

* Regulatory database
* Software development environment
* Validation and verification tools
* Collaboration and workflow management
* Integration with existing tools (optional)
* Automated testing and validation (optional)
* Reporting and analytics (optional)

### Out-of-Scope

The following features and functionalities are out-of-scope for this project:

* Development of medical device software
* Regulatory consulting services
* On-site implementation and support
* Custom development of workflows and integrations

### Assumptions and Dependencies

This project assumes the following:

* The regulatory database will be populated with relevant data from reputable sources.
* The software development environment will be compatible with popular development tools and frameworks.
* The validation and verification tools will be integrated with the regulatory database and software development environment.

This project depends on the following:

* Collaboration with regulatory affairs teams to ensure accuracy and completeness of the regulatory database.
* Input from software development teams to ensure the platform meets their needs.
* Integration with existing tools and frameworks to ensure seamless compatibility.
